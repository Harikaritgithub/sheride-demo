Why SheRide

Safety isn't a feature.
It's the product.

👩‍✈️
Women-Only Drivers
Every driver is Aadhaar-verified, background-checked, and voluntarily part of our trusted network. No exceptions.
🔐
Safety PIN System
Confirm your driver with a unique PIN before entering the vehicle. No PIN match — the ride doesn't start.
🆘
One-Tap SOS
Discreet SOS button immediately calls 112 and sends your live GPS location to all emergency contacts.
📍
Live Family Tracking
Auto-share trip details via WhatsApp. Family sees your route live. Auto "arrived safe" message on drop-off.
🌙
Midnight Mode
After 10pm, enhanced safety protocols activate automatically — extra check-ins and priority dispatch.
💰
Drivers Earn More
85% payout vs Ola/Uber's 75%. Better earnings attract better drivers. Win-win for safety and income.
